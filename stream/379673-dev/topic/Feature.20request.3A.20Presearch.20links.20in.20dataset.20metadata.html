Dieuwertje Bloemen (Sep 05 2024 at 06:49):

Yeah, we discussed it when we talked about showing the ORCIDs in the metadata block in a different way and if it's valuable to link to an author search. The idea is kinda based on a presentation I saw at Open Repositories this year by Herbert Van de Sompel and his colleagues (https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.12579438), where they mentioned the concept of "authorIDy" (https://signposting.org/authorIDy/) as part of signposting, I believe. Which is exactly that: being able to search on an author identifier in a repository, which would be a great help in harvesting relevant (metada)data for institutions who wish to catalogue all their output.
In the UX WG it came up when we talked about visualising links in the author metadata (link to their ORCID, to their ROR and possibly to such a search).
