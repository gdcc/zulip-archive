Guido Schmutz (Oct 09 2023 at 12:58):

FYI: I'm in the process of including Dataverse as one of the many services in Platys, a tool for generating flexible docker-compose stacks for PoCs and demos: https://github.com/TrivadisPF/platys-modern-data-platform. It's currently in the 1.17.0-preview version.
We are using the docker compose setup in a customer's project where we want to showcase Dataverse as the publication platform. Platys makes it easy to combine Dataverse with Keycloak, Minio, ..... and other services which are part of a modern data platform. But of course the goal is to use Kubernetes once we are sure that the customer is happy with a certain combination of tools ...
