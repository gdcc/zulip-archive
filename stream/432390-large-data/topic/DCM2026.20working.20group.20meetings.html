Julian Gautier (Feb 12 2026 at 16:07):

Hey everyone. Just passing along more info about the satellite meetings during this year's Dataverse Community Meeting that Dataverse working groups can use for meeting.

Working group leads interested in meeting in-person and online on May 12 can use the DCM2026 registration form to request a meeting for the working group.

On the registration form, WG leads will need to include a list of in-person attendees, who won't be let in if they haven't registered and aren't on the list, and are responsible for letting group members know about the May 12 meeting and for any marketing for new members.
