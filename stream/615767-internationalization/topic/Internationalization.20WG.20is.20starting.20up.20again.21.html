Internationalization Working Group 2.0 - we’re starting up again!

Welcome to Internationalization Working Group 2.0! We'll post updates in the Zulip #internationalization channel as we ramp up activities, so keep an eye out! Check out a summary of the proposed revitalization of the group, below. 

More context and information from the July 2026 Dataverse Community Meeting: DCM_July-2026_Internationalization_20260706 - Google Slides or check out the recording on Dataverse TV!

What will the Internationalization WG 2.0 look like?

In the new working group meetings, we’re hoping to discuss, share, and harness the power of the community to support internationalization activities:

  • Discuss and share workflows, technical implementations, etc.

  • New SPA interface and new internationalization process

  • Support for Dataverse installations at all stages of internationalization 

  • Peer sharing processes and tools for different languages and installations

  • Development of tools and standardized processes

Who can join?

All are welcome to attend and join the working group. We welcome participants with technical or linguistic expertise, and anyone interested in learning about translation and multilingual infrastructure.If you cannot join the meetings, feel free to tune in asynchronously in Zulip. Feel free to check out archived conversations in the ⁠Google group. 

What comes next?

We will share more information about scheduling meetings and other details as we restart the group. If you'd like to take a more hands-on active role in organizing this group, please let us know!

Visit the Internationalization working group page for more updates and information about upcoming meetings.
