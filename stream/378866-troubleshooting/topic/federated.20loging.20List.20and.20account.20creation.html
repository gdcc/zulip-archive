Good morning, Harvard and UNC use the InCommon Federation https://incommon.org/federation/ but that may not be what UCLA Dataverse is using. You either want to get JPL/NASA added to your IDP metadata feed, or you can "wedge" the JPL/NASA IDP metadata into your shibboleth2.xml via an include. Or if they support OIDC or OAuth2 you can add JPL/NASA's key and secret?
