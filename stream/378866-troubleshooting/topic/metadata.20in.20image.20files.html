Currently Dataverse extracts metadata from these formats:

  • tabular files: https://guides.dataverse.org/en/6.10.1/user/dataset-management.html#tabular-data-files
  • FITS (Astronomy format): https://guides.dataverse.org/en/6.10.1/user/dataset-management.html#astronomy-fits
  • NetCDF/HDF5: https://guides.dataverse.org/en/6.10.1/user/dataset-management.html#netcdf-and-hdf5

But not images, sorry. Please feel free to open a feature request! :smile:
