Juan (Mar 15 2024 at 09:47):

Hi @Lennard Golsch , You can create and modify groups from the Dataverse Community "Edit" menu. You need admin privileges in the community to do it.

When you create a group and assign specific roles to users into the selected Dataverse Community (create datasets / modify datsets,...)
