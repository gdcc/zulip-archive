Topic: PIDs for Projects Community Dialogue

Dimitri Szabo (Oct 07 2024 at 08:52):

As already sent in the google group, DataCite calls for members of the Life Sciences and Astronomy communities to join its Persistent Identifiers for Projects Community Dialogue, starting on Oct.15 10PM UTC.
It might be interesting for other research communities and installations too as the topics will include:

  • Contributors and creators
  • Spatial & temporal coverage
  • Rights, access & traditional knowledge
  • Funder and grant metadata
  • Connections to project outputs

More info and registration :
https://datacite.org/event/persistent-identifiers-for-projects-community-dialogue
