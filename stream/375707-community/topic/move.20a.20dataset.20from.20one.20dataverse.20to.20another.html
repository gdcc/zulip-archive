Sherry Lake (Sep 24 2024 at 15:59):

I think "linking" datasets is what you mean by "sharing"?
A dataset must have a primary "home", but if you link it to another dataverse (collection), then that dataset will show up in both collections when displaying dataverse (collection) datasets.

https://guides.dataverse.org/en/latest/user/dataverse-management.html#dataset-linking
