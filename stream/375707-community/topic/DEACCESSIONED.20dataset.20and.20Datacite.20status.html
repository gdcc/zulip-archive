Gwenaël Doux (Mar 11 2025 at 15:09):

Hi @luddaniel , I can only answer your first question. DataCite will not automatically change the DOI status from "Findable" to "Registered." At Cirad Dataverse, we have some datasets where all versions are deaccessioned, and I had to manually change the DOI status in DataCite Fabrica for these specific datasets.
