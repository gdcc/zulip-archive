ANNOUNCEMENT: we did tech tests today in Alumni Hall (if you want to know what room your presentation is in, see the agenda on the conference website).

What we discovered about multimedia in slides wrt remote attendees:

  • you CAN show videos with sound or live demos with sound from the presentation computer, and remote people can hear it
  • you CAN talk and remote people can hear it
  • you CANNOT do both at the same time!

In other words, audio can only be from one input at a time.

So if you want to talk over your website demonstration that has sound, or comment on videos you are playing, the remote people will only be able to hear one or the other audio tracks, not both.
