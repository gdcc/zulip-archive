Tilo Mathes (Dec 15 2025 at 13:35):

Thanks Phil, I'd probably revise my first hunch after looking at the DataCite metadata schema. There it is recommended to use the "Related Identifier" property with a corresponding relation (e.g. isPartOf or whatever makes most sense). I just noticed that "Related Identifier" in DataCite maps to "Related Publication" in dataverse, according to this crosswalk document: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1VAhZ83hKURX_4T-bOkn7XCb9h62Gr2gcJI1fWe4Rl4c/edit?gid=0#gid=0 . "Related Publication" feels a bit 'off' but it would put the information in the right place wrt to DataCite.

Happy to create an issue around this.

Julian Gautier (Dec 15 2025 at 16:29):

Hey all. Thanks for pinging me, Phil.

Tilo, like you wrote, I agree that using "Related Publication" would put the information in the right place with regard to DataCite, but that "Related Publication" field is meant to describe articles and other text-based publications, and it sounds like what RAiD identifiers should be used for would not be articles or other text-based publications. I saw that you didn't mention the "Related Publication" field in the GitHub issue you opened at https://github.com/IQSS/dataverse/issues/12045, but I just wanted to help reply to that point here.
